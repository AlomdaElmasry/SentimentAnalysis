Our Mission

In this project, we aim to design a web application that analyzes big volumes of Arabic reviews, social networks posts And tweets about specific product, Then, presents the results of this big data analytics job In a user friendly, understandable, and easily interpreted manner that can be easily used by different customers for different purposes.

And to make the user upload his training and test data And choose one or more algorithm to compare between their accuracy and test one of them if he need..
